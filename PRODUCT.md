# Product

## Register

product

## Users

VEX users are students, builders, and workers trying to start and finish focused blocks without friction. They open the app in low-energy, distracted, or momentum-seeking moments and need one clear next action, not a crowded productivity dashboard.

## Product Purpose

VEX is an adaptive productivity execution system. It starts with one real focus session, learns work rhythm over time, and reveals the right Home, Focus, Progress, and Profile surfaces around sessions, streaks, identity, and momentum.

## Brand Personality

Calm, premium, adaptive. VEX should feel intelligent and focused without hype, noisy gamification, or generic SaaS polish.

## Anti-references

Do not look dark cyberpunk, crypto, casino, shop, economy, social-guild, battle-pass, or generic glassmorphism template. Avoid oversized pastel blobs, low-contrast translucent text, nested card stacks, giant poster headings, and decorative glass that hides the actual task.

## Design Principles

1. Start-first clarity: every screen should make the next useful action obvious.
2. Intelligent glass: glass establishes hierarchy and touch, not decoration.
3. Real proof over fake rewards: progress, streak, focus score, and mastery reflect actual sessions.
4. Premium calm: light, mint, pearl, compact, legible, and tactile.
5. Feature discipline: active app surfaces must not revive archived shop, currency, or social pressure systems.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Default target is WCAG AA readable contrast, dark slate text over light glass, 44 point touch targets, reduced-motion support, accessible labels for all controls, and no interaction hidden behind color alone.
