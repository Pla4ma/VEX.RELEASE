## Day 3 "What VEX Learned" Design

**Display**: Medium-weight card on Home screen. Dismissable. Editable (tap observation to dismiss/keep).

**Structure**:
- Title: "What VEX learned"
- Observation (1-2 max)
- Confidence indicator (high/medium/low)
- "Dismiss" action
- "VEX is still learning..." fallback when confidence is low

**Examples**:
- "Your sessions started cleaner when the task was named first."
- "You completed more when your first block was under 20 minutes."
- "You dismissed evening nudges twice, so VEX will stay quieter then."
- "You finish sessions at ~25 minutes — VEX will suggest this length."
- Low confidence: "VEX is still learning your rhythm. Complete one more session to make this sharper."

## Day 7 Weekly Intelligence Design

**Display**: Full-screen or prominent Home card. Should feel like the first "wow" moment.

3. **Your best next session type** — recommended mode
4. **One suggested adjustment** — single concrete change
5. **One next action** — start-session CTA
6. **Deeper intelligence** — premium teaser (optional)
