Common Questions

How do I change my focus lane?

Tap the lane selector at the top of the home screen to switch between Study, Run, Project, or Clean.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through the App Store or Google Play Store on your device. Go to your device Settings → Subscriptions to manage or cancel.

How do I delete my account?

Open VEX → Settings → Account → Delete Account. Your data will be removed within 30 days.

How does the Focus Score work?

Your Focus Score is calculated from session consistency, streak length, grade quality, and lane variety. Complete sessions daily to improve it.
